DEAD KENNEDYS AT THE RIALTO 03/11/2016

Feb. 22, 2016

We’ve got a big night coming up in a couple of weeks. On March 11, 2016, we’re gonna be hitting up The Rialto, along with The Besmirchers, to open up with a really nice line up of both old and new punk bands. The legendary Dead Kennedys will be rolling through town that night and we’re sure they’re going to pack the whole place up. Let us know if you need any tickets.

You can contact us through Facebook at https://www.facebook.com/Hotchiks520 or shoot us an email to thehotchiks520@gmail.com. We’ll be more than happy to deliver the tickets straight to you.

Also touring with Dead Kennedys is The Freeze and Doll Skin, so make sure you make it to the show!

LIVE ALBUM IN THE WORKS

Feb. 22, 2016

Back in April of 2015, the opportunity for us to record a live album came our way and of course, we had to jump on it. At first, it seemed a little weird for a local punk band like us to even worry about doing something so unusual. We were in the process of figuring out what to do with the next full-length, so at first we were reluctant to play some of our newer songs for the live record, but the more we thought about it, the more excited we got.

Kenny was able to book us a show at Club XS and with the help of Tommy Rat, the preparations were in order. The show took place September 4th, 2015, giving us enough time to get some friends in other bands time to jump on the show and have everyone promoting the crap out of it. It didn’t take long for the free tickets to run out, so we ended up letting everyone and their mothers in.

The show was super fun. Kenny and Tommy Rat ended up working on it for the next few months, between their busy schedules, but finally we’ve got everything ready for you guys. Without further adieu, we’re happy to announce “Who Pissed Off The Sound Guy?”, available for download at Bandcamp February 29, 2016.
