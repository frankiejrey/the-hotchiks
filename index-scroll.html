The Rock June 8, 2014

It all started back in '09. I moved out to a suburb of Tucson, AZ because of a house fire. I hated being out there. I would spend every weekend in town skateboarding and hanging out with my best pals. One of them was Pablo. He mentioned that he played drums, but they were always jammed up in storage at his apartment. One fateful day, spring cleaning came around and we took the drums to my place. I only knew a couple of riffs and plucks on a guitar. Thankfully, my older brothers jammed, so I would practice on one of their old acoustic guitars at the time. When we set up Pablo's drums, he began to play and I couldn't believe what I was hearing. I immediately picked up my acoustic and plucked the few chords I knew. The magic that I felt in that room, on that day, changed my life. We decided to make a band.read full bio
